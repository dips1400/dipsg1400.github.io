About bigSplash
“You don’t take a photograph, you make it”
-Ansel Adams

Our DNA:

Worldwide,Opensource,
Diversity,Quality

At bigSplash, we are convinced that
the digital space is an invaluable tool
for bringing transparency and equity
to the art market.
We provide artists with the tools that
enable them to independently manage
sale of their works. Team is working
around the clock, getting their energy
to promoting bigSplash artists to a
global audience.

In showcasing artists around the
world, we also hope that to allow
art lovers and collectors a-like to
explore new artistic horizons, to
embrace new cultures and to be
inspired by the works of talented
artists that they would have ever
never come across if it wasn't for
bigSplash and the team.

Dipti

Co-Founder


I was inspried by the paintings, arts, and the

art lovers.
